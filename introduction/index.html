A Brief Introduction

With the world becoming increasingly reliant on technology, the use of artifical intelligence tools is sweeping every corner of the nation. From generating content to handling massive datasets, and automating repetitive tasks, AI is quickly taking on responsibilities to enhance and improve the lives of humans. The applications of AI are endless and span from healthcare, to finance, to entertainment and now, even education. For many students, it's become necessary tool for learning. But, have you every wondered how students are actually using AI? This project investigates AI use and its relation to educational level, age, satisfaction, and grades. We looked at how students in Pakistan are using AI in their educational lives to get a snapshot of AI usage in this everchanging landscape of technology.

What does the data look like?

Using a dataset from Kaggle created by Tanzeela Aftab, we explored real data from 2026 based on students in Pakistan. The dataset was designed with the intent to provide an educational opportunity to explore data trends, perform exploratory data analysis, and generate machine learning models. The data includes the following features:

  1. Unique Student ID
  2. Student Age in Years
  3. Student Gender
  4. Education Level
  5. City Location
  6. Main AI Tool Used
  7. AI Usage Purpose
  8. Impact on Grades
  9. Student Satisfaction Level
