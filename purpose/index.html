How is AI Being Used?

AI usage was recorded based on five categories: 'Coding', 'Homework', 'Learning', 'Research', and 'Writing'. Why students are using AI tools provides a fascinating look at how work can vary across educational levels as well as what areas students find AI to be the most supportive.

Among students at the 'School' level, 'Homework' was the main reason for AI use, with 'Coding' being a close second. 'Learning' was the least reported purpose for using AI, but not by a significant margin. The distribution differed significantly from those at the 'College' level, where students reported using AI most for 'Learning' and 'Research.' In fact, 'College' students reported 'Homework' the least for AI usage. Patterns for AI usage at the 'University' level shared similarities to both other educational levels. 'Homework' and 'Learning' were reported as the main purposes for use at the 'University' level, with 'Coding' and 'Writing' being the least reported. Overall, 'University' AI usage was relatively invariable.
