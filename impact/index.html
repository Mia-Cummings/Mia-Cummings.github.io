How is AI Affecting Students?

Arguably one of the most fascinating aspects of students' use of AI is its educational impact. We know that students across Pakistan are using numerous AI tools for various reasons, but what effect is it having on their experiences? Let's take a closer look at how students reported AI's impact on their grades.

Among 'College' level students, a majority of respondents saw no change in grades because of AI use. Very few saw improvement in grades and some saw a slight decline. For 'School' level students, the largest group had an improvement in their grades, some saw no change, and even less saw a slight decline. 'University' level students reported improved grades a majority of the time, with a small proportion indicating a slight decline and no change in grades. For all respondents, a majority of students reported either improvement or no change in grades. Interestingly, a nonsignificant number of students reported a decline in grades attributed to the use of AI. It's possible that the usage of AI is correlating to harder material or a period of academic struggle, which could lead the data to suggest AI is causing grades to decline, when in fact, it is not.

Most students reported having low satsifaction with AI tools, and the least number of students reported having high satisfaction with AI tools. It's interesting that despite low satisfaction, students continue to use AI tools to assist them with work. It appears that the sentiment around AI tools is leaning negative among students, which is intriguing, considering its widespread use.
